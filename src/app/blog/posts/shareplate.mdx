# SharePlate (Mobile App Development)

**Group Project | Android Studio, Figma, Java, XML**  
*Oct 2024 - Dec 2024*

Designed and prototyped a mobile platform enabling users to give away or request food and household items within their community.

Emphasized user-friendly design, incorporating geolocation, category filters, and interactive request/offer flows.

Focused on solving food waste and accessibility issues by fostering local sharing through intuitive UI/UX.
