# Credit Limit Prediction (Machine Learning)

**Group Project | Python, Google Colab**  
*Mar 2024 - Jun 2024*

Built a model to predict credit card limits using a dataset of 7,298 entries with 14 features.

Applied preprocessing (outlier removal, scaling, encoding) and EDA.

Compared multiple models; Random Forest performed best with RMSE 0.48.
